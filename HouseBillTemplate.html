SECTION 108. SEVERABILITY

  1. SEVERABILITY. — If any provision of this Act, or the application thereof to any person or circumstance, is held invalid, the remainder of this Act and its application to other persons or circumstances shall not be affected.

  1. SUPREMACY. — Any existing provision of law which contradicts this Act shall be considered null and void for the purposes of interpreting this legislation.

  1. EFFECTIVE DATE. — The provisions of this Act shall be effective following this Act’s enactment.
